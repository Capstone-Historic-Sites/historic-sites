Persona

Name: Brian Mayng
Gender: Male
Age: 47
Profession: Dentist

Technology:

  • iPhone Xr, used for staying in quick touch with relatives while out on vacation.
  • Nikon D3400, as a tourist, Brian spent a good amount of money on a camera to take decent photos of all the sites he visits.
  • MacBook Pro 13in, Brian carries a MacBook with him to upload his pictures onto his hard drive when his camera runs out of memory.
  • Brian is not a power user, but is able to use his iPhone and MacBook with enough proficiency to capture and save pictures of his travels.
  • Brian is very satisfied with Apple technology and the quality of the photos that his camera takes.

Attitudes & Needs:

  • Needs to have access to every historical site in Albuquerque, as well as other cities in New Mexico.
  • Wants to be able to see a list of all the sites he can visit.
  • Would like information about the site before he gets there, like cost/amenities/hours of operation.
  • Would like general directions to the site.
  • Likes ease of use (A functional/helpful search bar and the ability to filter sites by interest).

Frustrations:

  • Hates bad customer service/bad website service.
  • Dislikes slow websites.
  • Doesn't like to visit websites without easy to use features.

User Story

As a tourist/casual user, I want to search historic sites in Albuquerque.

Use Case & Interaction Flow

Title: A trip to Old Town
Description: Casual user Brian, wants to sightsee around Albuquerque, and chooses to pick something close to his hotel to start off. The historical sites website shows Old Town appearing close by and he decides to explore the landmark.
Name & Role: Brian Mayng, tourist
Usage Preconditions:Brian wants to search for historical sites that relate to Billy the Kid.
Usage Postcondition:Brian has a travel list of all the historical sites with significance relating to Billy the Kid.

Interaction Flow:

  • Brian opens his laptop and visits the historical sites web page.
  • Server fullfils request and loads the historical sites web page.
  • He finds and clicks on the 'site locator' feature on the navigation bar.
  • Drop down list of historical sites appears on the page.
  • Brian clicks on the filter button at the top of the drop down menu/
  • Brian applies the filter to search for sites within Albuquerque.
  • Drop down list then collapses in size to show only the historical sites within the city of Albuquerque.
  • Brian clicks on the filter button again.
  • He applies a 'Billy the Kid' tag on the search results.
  • Search results collapse down in size to show all historical sites relating to Billy the Kid.
  • He then clicks on the options button next to a historical site.
  • He adds the site to his custom list of sites to visit within Albuquerque relating to Billy the Kid.
  • He repeats this for every site in the filtered search results.
  • Server processes his requests and adds all the sites to his travel list.
  • Brian closes the drop down menu.

Frequency of Use: Semi- regular use, 3-4 times a day when on vacation (goes one vacation twice a year)
