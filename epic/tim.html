Persona

Name: Tim Wild
Gender: Male
Age: 31
Profession: Accountant
Technology: iPhone11, HP Spectre Laptop with Windows 10

Attitudes & Needs:

  • Has a fondness for traveling to different historical places.
  • Loves the outdoors and is a bit of a history buff. Wants to visit places he has read about.
  • Wants to be able to see many locations of historic places to visit.
  • Needs a way to save locations he wants to visit in the future.
  • Would like to see what kind of amenities an historic site has and a brief overview of the place.

Frustrations:

  • Not being able to easily find historical sites on the web.
  • Navigating through poorly designed historic sites web pages.
  • Lack of contact forms to offer suggestions of places to add to a site.

User Story

As a registered user, I want to add historic sites to my travel list.

Use Case & Interaction Flow

Title: Saving historical sites to personal travel list.
Description: Tim finds locations he wants to visit and saves them to his travel list on his account so that he can visit them later.
Name and Role: Tim, a registered user and history buff who enjoys visiting historic locations.
Usage Preconditions: Has an account and is logged into the site.
Usage Postconditions: Tim has a list of locations he wants to visit on his travel list.

Interaction Flow:

  • Tim enters historic site's website into a browser of his choice.
  • The server returns with the historic site's homepage.
  • Tim will sign into his account.
  • Server takes you to your account.
  • Tim will click on the travel list icon.
  • Options pop down.
  • Tim will select the add icon.
  • Server responds by opening the search page with locations to select.
  • Tim will select the places he will like to visit.
  • Server responds by saving Tim's selection of places.
  • Tim is now looking through his newly created travel list with places he would like to visit.

Frequency of Use: 1-2 times a week.
