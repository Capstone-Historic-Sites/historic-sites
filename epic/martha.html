Persona

Name: Martha Woods
Gender: Female
Age: 34
Profession: Paralegal at a law firm
Technology: Latest iPhone and MacBook Pro

Attitudes & Needs:

  • Member of the four corners historical society
  • Resident of Colorado
  • Indigenous individual
  • Joyful, focused, very intentional and driven.
  • Loves seeing a polished & functional site.
  • As a registered user of a historical society, Martha would like to utilize Historic Sites web functionality to upload some additions to the site which are important to her ancestors cultural past.

Frustrations:

  • Hates running into buggy sites and having an upload fail due to a site's database not being properly debugged.
  • Frustrated by the lack of data bases containing historically accurate information about her culture & ancestral past

User Story

As a registered user and part of a historical society, I want to upload a historical site to the database.

Use Case & Interaction Flow:

Title: Registered user & apart of an organization or historical society
Description: Martha wants to upload a couple data sources about a couple missing historically significant areas.
Name & Role: Martha, registered user on the site and member of a historical society.
Usage Preconditions: Having already created an account.
Usage Postconditions: The upload is successful the site displays the newly uploaded content.

Interaction Flow:

  • Martha enters the page and clicks the upload function.
  • The site redirects her to the upload page.
  • Martha is then directed to upload her contribution, she adds several photos of the site, a description, time period and important details such as the location. Additionally, she adds a label titled, "Native American Ruins." She then hits upload.
  • The site pops up a "thank you for your contribution" message upon successful upload.

Frequency of Use: 2-4 times per month
